## Notes
- Pin 5 (SW): NC marker — no connect in normal (non-fast-lock) mode per datasheet
- 6 VCC supply pins each decoupled with 100nF + 1µF (C1–C12)
- 3 mandatory bias caps: C13 NOISE_FILT, C14 BIAS_FILT, C15 REG (all 1µF to GND)
- REF_IN (pin 29) AC-coupled via C16 (100nF) — internal bias at VCC_DIG/2
- 3rd-order passive loop filter: C17 (820pF) shunt + R5 (250Ω) series + C18 (12nF) ∥ R6 (120Ω) + C19 (100nF) shunts at VTUNE
- CE (pin 4) and RFOUT_EN (pin 26) pulled high via 10kΩ (R2, R3)
- LD (pin 25) pull-up R4 (10kΩ) for open-drain analog lock-detect mode
- All 4 open-collector RF outputs biased via 27nH chokes (L1–L4) from VCC, terminated into 50Ω (R7–R10)
- EP (pin 33) connected to GND — solder to ground plane with via array
